Privacy Policy – Data Collection, Storage & Usage

1. What Data We Collect

We collect and store the following data:

  • Username: Used to identify players in the game.
  • Game Scores: Used to generate leaderboards and player statistics.
  • Account Creation Time: Logged to track user registration dates.

2. Why We Collect This Data

  • To construct leaderboards and display player rankings.
  • To generate game statistics and improve player experience.
  • To analyze user engagement and enhance game features.

3. Where Your Data is Stored

All collected data is securely stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union (EU).

For more details, see Supabase’s privacy policy.

4. How Long We Store Your Data

Your data will be stored as long as your account remains active. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Modify: Update your username if necessary.
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop data collection by deleting your account.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at 404apps.dev@gmail.com or use the Delete My Data option in the game settings.
